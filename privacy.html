Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

StatusSavePro helps you save WhatsApp statuses to your device. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your choices. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

📂 Data we access

The app reads media from WhatsApp status folders on your device when you grant storage access. This lets us show status photos and videos in the app so you can preview and save them.

When you save a status, files are written locally to:

  • Photos: Pictures/StatusSavePro
  • Videos: Movies/StatusSavePro

🔒 Local storage only

Status previews, thumbnails, and downloads stay on your phone. We do not upload your statuses, contacts, messages, or saved files to our servers. We do not sell your personal information.

🛡️ Permissions

Storage permissions are used only to:

  • List WhatsApp status media folders you have already viewed in WhatsApp
  • Save media you explicitly choose to your gallery
  • Manage files in your in-app Saved library

You can revoke storage access at any time in Android Settings. Without permission, the app cannot browse or save statuses, but previously saved files on your device remain yours.

📤 Sharing & third-party apps

When you tap Share or Repost, StatusSavePro uses Android's system share sheet. The app you select (e.g. WhatsApp, Instagram, Gmail) handles that content under its own privacy policy. We do not control how third-party apps use shared files.

ℹ️ Third-party services & affiliation

StatusSavePro is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by WhatsApp or Meta Platforms, Inc. WhatsApp is a trademark of Meta Platforms, Inc.

The app does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that collect your status content. If we add optional services in the future (for example, crash reporting), we will update this policy.

👤 Children's privacy

StatusSavePro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, contact us and we will address it promptly.

🔄 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the revised policy.
